# EOS Defence Systems - Products and Specifications

**Company:** Electro Optic Systems (EOS), Symonston, ACT, Australia
**Role in LAND 156:** Key partner under LOE1 providing Australian effector systems (kinetic and directed energy)
**Website:** [eos-aus.com](https://eos-aus.com)

EOS has delivered more than 2,500 remote weapon stations worldwide. EOS Defense Systems USA operates as the company's US hub since 2018. All major products are ITAR-free.

---

## 1. Slinger - Counter-UAS Effector

The Slinger is a lightweight hard-kill counter-UAS weapon system built on the R400 lineage, introduced in May 2023. It is centred on a 30x113 mm chain gun with specialised fragmentation ammunition optimised for small unmanned aircraft.

### Specifications

| Parameter | Value |
|---|---|
| **Weapon** | M230LF 30x113 mm chain gun |
| **Ammunition** | 150 rounds RF proximity-fused HE/fragmentation (30x113 mm) |
| **Rate of Fire** | Selectable: single-shot, 100 rpm, or 200 rpm |
| **System Weight** | 376 kg (configuration with M230LF and 150 ready rounds) |
| **Engagement Range** | Demonstrated beyond 800 m in live fire |
| **Stabilisation** | Four-axis stabilised mount (on-the-move fires) |
| **Sighting** | Four-axis day and thermal sight; sensor unit moves independently of gun in elevation and azimuth |
| **Radar** | Integrated 4D electronically steered radar (Echodyne MESA) |
| **Cost per Engagement** | $155 - $1,550 |
| **Unit Cost** | Less than $1.55 million |

### Key Features

- Sub-milliradian pointing precision for extreme accuracy against low-RCS quadcopters
- AI-enabled Aided Target Recognition (ATR) with selectable levels of autonomy
- Differentiates between birds and drones; identifies friendly vs hostile drones
- Can operate independently with passive sensors or integrate with external sensor cueing
- Open-architecture system that plugs into broader air-defence and battle-management networks

### Deployments

- 160 units ordered for Ukraine (110 on M113 APCs, 50 on Practika 4x4 MRAPs) - announced September 2023

## 2. R400 - Remote Weapon Station

The R400 provides lightweight 30 mm lethality with precise mobile engagements up to 2 km.

### Specifications

| Parameter | Value |
|---|---|
| **System Weight** | Under 400 kg (30x113 mm configuration) |
| **Primary Weapon** | M230LF 30x113 mm cannon |
| **Alternative Weapons** | 7.62 mm MG, 12.7 mm HMG, 40 mm AGL |
| **Missile Support** | Javelin, Stinger, Spike (marine variant); extends engagement to 4,000 m |
| **Stabilisation** | Three- to four-axis stabilisation, sub-milliradian precision |
| **Engagement Range** | Up to 2 km (mobile engagements) |
| **Dual Weapon** | Yes, with integrated ballistic computer and video tracking |
| **Weapon Change Time** | Less than 1 hour (modular plug-and-play) |
| **Target Types** | Small to medium UAS/drones, STANAG Level 3 protected vehicles |

### Deployments

- In service across Australia, North America, Europe, and South-East Asia
- Selected for integration on Australia's AS21 Redback IFV under LAND 400 Phase 3

## 4. Apollo - High Energy Laser Weapon

Apollo is a scalable high-energy laser air-defence system designed for counter-UAS and counter-swarm operations.

### Specifications

| Parameter | Value |
|---|---|
| **Laser Power** | 50 kW to 150 kW (scalable) |
| **Hard-Kill Range** | 50 m to 3 km |
| **Optical Sensor Denial Range** | 50 m to 15 km |
| **Target Types** | Group 1-3 UAS |
| **Kill Rate** | More than 20 Group 1 UAS per minute (at 150 kW) |
| **Slew Speed** | 60 degrees in ~700 ms |
| **Target Lock Time** | ~600 ms |
| **Time to Neutralise** | ~1.3 seconds for Group 1 UAS at 50 kW |
| **Internal Magazine** | Over 200 UAS kills (independent operation) |
| **Continuous Fire** | Yes, with external electrical power and cooling |
| **Packaging** | 20-foot ISO container; option for 8x8 vehicle chassis |
| **Setup Time** | Under 2 hours by experienced crew |
| **Integration** | NATO air defence C2 systems; theatre-level IADS |
| **ITAR Status** | ITAR-free; all technology and IP wholly owned by EOS |

### Contracts

- EUR 71.4 million (USD 84 million) order from undisclosed European NATO member (delivery 2025-2028)
- USD 80 million conditional agreement with South Korean customer (100 kW class)

## 5. Titanis - Integrated Counter-UAS System

Titanis is a fully integrated, layered counter-drone system combining soft-kill, hard-kill, and directed energy capabilities.

### Specifications

| Parameter | Value |
|---|---|
| **System Type** | Fully integrated layered C-UAS (soft-kill, hard-kill, directed energy) |
| **Response Time** | 1-4 seconds (detect-to-defeat) |
| **Kinetic Options** | 7.62 mm MG (minimum) |
| **Directed Energy** | Up to 54 kW high energy laser |
| **DE Swarm Defence** | 35 kW directed energy component |
| **Detection Sensors** | Software-defined, multi-mission 4D AESA pulse Doppler radar |
| **Passive Detection** | Passive RF detection |
| **Tracking** | High-precision infrared and daylight cameras with advanced video tracking |
| **Soft-Kill** | Non-kinetic RF inhibitor |
| **Scalability** | Individual vehicles to large facility protection |
| **Engagement Sequence** | Detect (radar/RF) -> Track (EO/IR) -> Soft-kill (RF inhibitor) -> Hard-kill (kinetic/laser if RF inhibitor ineffective) |
