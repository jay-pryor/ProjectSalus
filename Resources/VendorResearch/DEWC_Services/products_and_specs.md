# DEWC Services - Electronic Warfare Products and Capabilities

## Company Overview

DEWC Services is a sovereign Australian C4ISREW specialist, founded in 2011 by ADF veteran Allan Dundas (a graduate of the Australian Defence Force Academy and electronic warfare specialist). Headquartered in South Australia with additional offices in the Australian Capital Territory, DEWC has grown to a team of approximately 100 C4ISREW specialists.

DEWC is regarded as Australia's foremost specialists in applied C4ISREW expertise, having delivered over 200 Defence projects.

---

## DEWC Group Structure

The DEWC Group comprises three entities:

| Entity | Focus |

## Spectrum Management and Security

### KBR Partnership

DEWC has partnered with KBR to collaborate on spectrum security technology in support of the ADF. The partnership leverages KBR's history in space and spectrum management, protection, and assurance, transferring knowledge and capabilities to Australia.

Scope of Electromagnetic Spectrum Operations includes:

- Assured communication links
- Precision navigation and timing
- Spectrum management
- Spectrum exploitation

## Counter-UAS Relevance

DEWC Services does not publicly market a specific off-the-shelf counter-UAS hardware product. However, their capabilities are highly relevant to the counter-UAS domain:

| Capability | Counter-UAS Application |
|---|---|
| **RF Countermeasures (FP-ECM)** | Drone RF link jamming and disruption |
| **EW Library Development** | Threat characterisation of drone control signals |
| **Applied Machine Learning** | AI-based signal classification for drone detection |
| **Bespoke Systems Development** | Custom C-UAS electronic warfare solutions |
| **Spectrum Management** | Detection of unauthorised drone RF emissions |
| **Systems Integration** | Integration of EW effectors into broader C-UAS architectures |

DEWC's Counter-IED experience with RF electronic countermeasures is directly transferable to counter-UAS RF jamming applications, as both involve detecting and disrupting hostile radio-frequency signals.
