Kill-Chain Timeline Analysis

The D-T-I-D-E-A (Detect–Track–Identify–Decide–Engage–Assess) kill-chain model determines whether the defender can complete the engagement sequence before the drone reaches the protected asset. A positive margin indicates the kill chain is feasible; negative margin means the drone arrives before the chain completes.
